Thank you for using songjam.space, a platform that transforms X (Twitter) Spaces into accessible text-to-speech and offers AI-powered analysis of space listeners. Your privacy is important to us.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect your email address when you log in to our service.

We do not collect any other personal information such as name, phone number, or address.

2. How We Use Your Email

We use your email address:

We do not send marketing emails or newsletters.

3. How We Store Your Information

Your email address is securely stored in our backend system, which is protected by industry-standard security practices. We do not sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party tools (e.g., Firebase, Google Login) to manage authentication or analytics. These providers have their own privacy policies.

5. Data Retention

We retain your email address for as long as you have an active account with us. You can request deletion at any time.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under laws like GDPR or CCPA, including:

  • Accessing your data
  • Requesting correction or deletion
  • Withdrawing consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at team@songjam.space.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in the future. If we do, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Significant changes will be communicated via email or through our website.
